Built For The Wild

Ad Campaign for Toyo Tires

Concept/Art Direction/Design

Pan-European print ad campaign for Toyo.

For this campaign Toyo wanted to focus on the brand’s 3 main products: Open country for 4x4 and off-road, Proxes for the speed/races and Snowprox for the winter tires.

We built 3 vivid universes, each championed by an iconic hero animal.

Rhino was selected for Off-road tires, Cheetah for Speed tires and the Wolf for their Winter tire range. Emphasis was placed on the poise and attitude of each animal as we wanted to focus on their instinctive and primal characteristics.
